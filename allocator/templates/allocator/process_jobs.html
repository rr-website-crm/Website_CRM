Temperature Check Information

What is Temperature Check?

Temperature check matches the AI summary uploaded by marketing team with the final file provided by the writer to ensure content consistency and quality.

Passing Criteria

The temperature score must meet the minimum threshold (typically 70%) for the task to be approved and moved to the next stage.

Process Team Role

Process team members upload the final file and verify that the temperature check passes before proceeding to the decoration phase.
